yellow- don't fire, walk forward (walk speed 1, fire speed 0)
brown- fire slowly (walk speed 1, fire speed 1)
green- fire medium, walk faster than yellow/brown/gray (walk speed 2, fire speed 1)
pink- walk same speed as green, fire fast (walk speed 2, fire speed 2)
blue- walk 1.5x green/blue, fire fast (walk speed 3, fire speed 2)
beige- walk 2x green/blue, fire fast (walk speed 4, fire speed 2)
purple- walk same speed as beige, fire 1.5x faster (walk speed 4, fire speed 3)

scoring-
50 points per robot killed
if all robots in a room are killed, 10 bonus points per robot
extra life at 1000 points
